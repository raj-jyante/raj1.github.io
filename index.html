About MTC :

MAHARASTRA TREKKING COMPANY ( MTC ) is a trekking company dedicated to exploring the Western Ghats region of Maharashtra. They specialize in organizing treks to lesser-known destinations, hidden gems, and offbeat trails, allowing adventurers to discover the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the Sahyadri mountain range. With knowledgeable guides, personalized service, and a commitment to sustainable tourism practices, Trek the Sahyadris offers unique and unforgettable trekking experiences for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.
